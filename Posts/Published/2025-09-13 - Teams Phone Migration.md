Out with the old, in with the new.

My amazing team did what everyone said couldn't be done. They migrated over 14,000 users around the world from a Cisco UC phone system over to Microsoft Teams Phone in under 3 months. We also implemented a "Softphone first" philosophy for everyone but our Executive Assistants and Front Desk personnel. I am so proud of them and everyone else across our IT organization that played a part in this project.
